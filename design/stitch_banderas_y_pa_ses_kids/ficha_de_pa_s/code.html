Meaning of the Flag

The national flag of Japan is officially called Nisshōki ("Sun-mark flag"), but is more commonly known as Hinomaru ("Circle of the Sun").

It features a red circle representing the sun in the center of a white field. White represents honesty, integrity, and purity, while the red circle represents the sun, as Japan is famously known as the "Land of the Rising Sun." This imagery connects the nation to the sun goddess Amaterasu, the mythological founder of Japan.
